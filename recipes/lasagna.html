Description

lasanga, is made with two sheets. Inside the sheets, are vegetables, cheese, sauce and meat

Ingredients

    FOR THE SAUCE :

  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 medium red onions, finely diced
  • 2 large cloves minced garlic
  • 8 ounces pancetta, diced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups good wine, preferabley Italian
  • 2 28-ounce cans Italian plum tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 3/4 pound ground sirloin
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated pecorino Romano
  • 2 eggs

  • FOR THE LASAGNA

  • 1 15-ounce container ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups freshly grated pecorino Romano
  • 1 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 pound mozzarella, grated
  • 16 sheets fresh lasagna noodles, preferabley Antica Pasteria
